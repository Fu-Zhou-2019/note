**[Jensen's Inequality](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jensen%27s_inequality)**

$$
\phi (E[X]) \le E[\phi(X)]
$$

if $$\phi$$ is a convex function, a simple example:  X~U(0,1) and $$\phi(x) = x^{2}$$.

**[Planar and Radial Flows]()**

Planar Flow:
$$
g(x) = x + u h(w^{T}x +b)
$$

An extension based on Planar flow is Sylvester flow:
$$
g(x) = x + U h(W^{T}x +b)
$$
where U and W are DxM matrices

Radial Flows:

$$
g(x) = x + \frac{\beta}{\alpha + ||x-x_{0}||}(x-x_{0})
$$
